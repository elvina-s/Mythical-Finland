About us

Mythical Finland is a specialist tour operator providing thematical tours around Finland and was founded in The Netherlands in 2020.

Mythical Finland is about Scandinavia, local mythology and a solitude with the wild nature. Moreover, we focus on sustainability. Thus, we provide eco-friendly activities and accommodations.

As a part of our thematical travels, we gift a special book to our travelers. It is both a folklore book and a map of the trip itinerary. Thus, our travellers get immersed into the theme: Finnish fairy tales and myths.
